About

Trend2Short AI helps creators move from trends to publish-ready short content faster.

Trend2Short AI is an experimental creator tool designed to turn trending topics into structured short-form content ideas for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.

What The MVP Does

The current MVP helps creators transform a trend into a video idea, hook, short script, caption, and hashtags inside a local browser experience.

Who It Is For

It is built for creators, marketers, and short-form content teams who want a simple way to explore content angles before publishing.

Current Scope

This version is intentionally lightweight. It works with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only, without a backend, external APIs, or third-party libraries.

Future Direction

The interface is structured to evolve into a more complete SaaS platform later, including richer templates, account features, and more advanced content workflows.
