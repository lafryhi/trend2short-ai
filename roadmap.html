Roadmap

What the MVP already does, and what comes next.

Trend2Short AI is intentionally lightweight today. The roadmap below shows the current creator workflow, the AI integration path, and the product areas planned for future SaaS expansion.

Current Features

  • Demo generator for hooks, video ideas, scripts, captions, hashtags, and CTAs
  • History with search, filters, pinning, duplication, and reload
  • Examples library for discovery and SEO
  • Local-only storage with no backend

Planned Features

The next phase focuses on moving from browser-only demo workflows to production-ready AI generation, collaboration, export, and planning tools.

AI Integration

The new AI service layer will support real providers later, with provider-specific adapters and safer response validation.

Team Accounts

Shared workspaces, collaborator roles, and centralized content workflows are part of the longer-term SaaS direction.

Content Calendar

Planned scheduling views will help creators organize upcoming short-form topics, themes, and publishing cadence.

Export to Notion

Future export flows will allow users to move generated ideas and scripts into team planning systems with less manual work.

Export to Google Docs

Document export is planned for creators who need editable drafts, review loops, and longer form collaboration.

Analytics

Later versions may surface trend velocity, output usage, and performance-oriented insights for creators and marketing teams.
